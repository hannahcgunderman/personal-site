What is geography?

When I tell folks that I am a geographer, their first response is usually "So, you probably know the capital of (insert any country)?" or "Oh, so like maps and stuff?" Geography is so much more than memorizing placenames and locating places on the Earth's surface! Geographers merge local and global perspectives to address today's social, economic, environmental, and cultural challenges.
